Lasagna

Recipe credit to SimplyRecipes

A wholesome favourite for all the family

Description

This lasagna is a gooey, cheesy, easy to make crowd pleaser. Full of flavour and perfect for a cosy night in.

Ingredients

Lasagna sauce

  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 large bell pepper, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g tomato passata
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp oregano
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp mixed herbs
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste

To assemble

  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 1 tub ricotta cheese
  • 1 ball mozzarella
  • 100g fresh parmesan

Method

  1. Put a large pot of salted water on the stovetop on high heat. Prepare the sauce whilst the water is heating
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan on medium heat, with the olive oil. Remove and drain the beef with a slotted spoon.
  3. Add the bell pepper, onions and garlic to the skillet. Fry off for 4-5 minutes until soft. Add the mince and stir to combine. Cook over low heat for a further 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer to a large pot, add the crushed tomatoes, passata, and tomato paste. Add the herbs and spices to taste. Sprinkle with red wine vinegar and then simmer for 15 minutes, stirring often. Remove from the heat
  5. Boil the lasagna sheets until al dente, then drain and rinse with cool water. Preheat the over to 180 degrees.
  6. Coat a baking dish in a little olive oil and then layer your lasagna with the sauce and cooked lasagna sheets. In between layers add a mix of the ricotta, mozzarella and parmesan.
  7. Cover with foil, bake in the oven for 40 mins, and then allow to cool slightly before serving.
