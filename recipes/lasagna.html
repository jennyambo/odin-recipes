Lasagna

A wholesome favourite for all the family

Description

This lasagna is a gooey, cheesy, easy to make crowd pleaser. Full of flavour and perfect for a cosy night in

Ingredients

Lasagna sauce

  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 large bell pepper, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g tomato passata
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp oregano
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp mixed herbs
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste

To assemble

  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 1 tub ricotta cheese
  • 1 ball mozzarella
  • 100g fresh parmesan
